LATTE

Caffe latte is a coffee drink madewith espresso and steamed milk. The word comes from the Italian
caffè e latte, caffelatte or caffellatte
which means ‘’ coffee & milk ‘’.

MOCACCINO

Mocaccino is a chocolate-flavoured
variant ofa caffè latte besed on
espresso and hot milk but with
added chocolate and
sweetener.

MACCHIATO

Caffè macchiato , sometimes           
called espresso macchiato,
is an espresso coffee
drink with a small amount
of milk, usually foamed.

CAPPUCCINO

Cappuccino is a latte made           
with more foam than steamed        
milk, often with a sprinkle of
cocoa powder or cinnamon
on top.

FLAT WHITE

A flat white is a coffee drink            
consisting of espresso with
microfoam (steamed milk with
small, fine bubbles and a
glossy or velvety consistency).

AMERICANO

Caffè Americano is a type of
coffee drink prepared by diluting
an espresso with hot water, giving  
it a similar strength to, but
different flavor.

DOUBLE ESPRESSO

Doppio espresso is a double
shot which is extracted using
double the amount of ground
coffee in a larger-sized portafilter   
basket.

ESPRESSO

Caffe espresso is a coffee-brewing  
method , in which a small amount
of nearly boiling water is forced
under 9 bars of pressure through
finely-ground coffee beans.
